Company History

NGM is a specialist law firm focusing on tax. It was founded in 2012 by three former tax partners at CMS who have 75 years of combined expertise. We are equally comfortable working with fellow professional advisors or directly with clients.

Why Choose Us?

Because what you see is what you get and the advice you receive will be provided by senior and experienced practitioners. We are responsive and pragmatic and, if you would prefer, we are happy to work on a fixed fee basis which is fair and transparent.

Working With Other Advisors

We know that many firms are missing or have limited specialist sectors. It’s therefore no surprise that we frequently take instructions from other professional advisors seeking in depth tax advice for their clients.

We will solve complex tax issues for your clients. By incorporating our technical advice into your wider recommendations, you can expand your firm’s range of services and unlock new business opportunities.

No transaction is too big. We have access to a number of senior experienced tax practitioners who can assist us if required. We can become part of your team as your partner, not as an additional overhead.

NGM Private

Are you struggling to understand how to pass on your assets to your children? Do you have a complicated estate with properties abroad? You’re in good company.

We recognise that there is increasing demand for expert tax advice by high net worth individuals and their families. That’s why we set up NGM Private in 2018.

Tax affairs are challenging. But exposure can be minimised with the right advice. Our extensive experience and expertise will deliver pragmatic solutions to your tax affairs. We are responsive and, if you would prefer, are happy to work on a fixed fee basis which is fair and transparent.

No hidden costs. No nasty surprises. Just really good, coherent, specialist tax advice.

Fee Structure

We are acutely aware that cost matters to all of our clients. When offering tax assistance to fellow professional advisors, or directly to our corporate and private clients, we can work on a fixed fee basis, if preferred, which is fair and transparent. Our flexible approach to pricing means that you don’t have to worry about escalating fees. There are no hidden costs and no nasty surprises. Just really good, coherent, specialist tax advice.
